Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Introduction

Resilience: Anger Management AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS mobile application.

By using Resilience, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our app.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Email Address: Collected via Sign in with Apple for account creation and authentication
  • User ID: A unique, anonymized identifier for your account
  • Authentication Tokens: Secure tokens for maintaining your login session

2. Usage Data

  • AI Chat Messages: Your conversations with the AI emotional coach are stored to provide personalized support and track your progress
  • Journal Entries: Any entries you create in the app's journaling feature
  • Exercise Completion: Records of breathing exercises and other wellness activities you complete
  • Usage Statistics: Daily message counts, feature usage, session duration
  • Progress Data: Emotional wellness insights and patterns derived from your app usage

3. Subscription Information

  • Subscription Tier: Which plan you're subscribed to (Standard, Pro, or Free)
  • Purchase History: Transaction IDs and dates (processed through Apple)
  • Subscription Status: Active, expired, or trial status
  • Payment Information: We do NOT collect payment information directly; all payments are processed securely by Apple through the App Store

4. Technical and Device Information

  • Device Type: iPhone or iPad model
  • Operating System: iOS version
  • App Version: Which version of Resilience you're using
  • Crash Logs: Technical data about app crashes to help us fix bugs
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized usage patterns to improve app features

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide AI Chat Services: To deliver personalized emotional support and coaching through our AI-powered chat
  • Track Your Progress: To show you insights about your emotional wellness journey
  • Manage Your Account: To maintain your subscription, authenticate your identity, and provide account support
  • Improve Our Services: To analyze usage patterns and improve app features and performance
  • Process Subscriptions: To manage your subscription tier and daily message limits
  • Send Notifications: To send you important updates about your account or app changes (only if you've enabled notifications)
  • Comply with Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

How We Store and Protect Your Information

Data Storage

  • Firebase/Firestore: Your account data, messages, and progress are securely stored using Google Firebase and Firestore databases
  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Data stored in our databases is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
  • Cloud Functions: We use Firebase Cloud Functions for secure server-side processing

Security Measures

  • Industry-standard security protocols and encryption
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Strict access controls to your data
  • Secure authentication through Firebase Auth and Sign in with Apple

Note: While we implement strong security measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected

  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (not applicable as we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
