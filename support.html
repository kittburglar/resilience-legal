Frequently Asked Questions

How many AI messages do I get for free?
Free users receive 2 AI coaching messages per day. Upgrade to Premium for longer conversations.
How do I manage my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID:
  1. Open Settings on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select Resilience AI
  5. Manage or cancel your subscription
How do I restore my purchase?
Open the app, tap the subscription button, then tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the screen.
When does my daily message limit reset?
Your free messages reset daily at midnight in your local timezone.
Is my data private and secure?
Yes. All conversations are encrypted and private. We never sell your personal information. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
What if I'm experiencing a mental health crisis?
Resilience AI is not a substitute for professional care. If you're in crisis:
  • US: Call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
  • Text "HELLO" to 741741 (Crisis Text Line)
  • Call 911 or visit your nearest emergency room
Can I get a refund?
Refund requests are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund for your subscription.
